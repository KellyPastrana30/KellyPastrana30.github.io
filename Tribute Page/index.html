Kobe Bryant

The legendary basketball player

Here's a time line of Kobe Bryant's life:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996 - Drafted by the Charlotte Hornets and traded to the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 1997 - Named NBA Slam Dunk Champion
  • 2000 - Wins his first NBA Championship with the Lakers
  • 2001 - Wins his second NBA Championship with the Lakers
  • 2002 - Wins his third NBA Championship with the Lakers
  • 2006 - Scores a career-high 81 points against the Toronto Raptors
  • 2008 - Wins his only NBA MVP award
  • 2009 - Wins his fourth NBA Championship with the Lakers
  • 2010 - Wins his fifth NBA Championship with the Lakers
  • 2016 - Retires from the NBA after a 20-year career
  • 2020 - Posthumously inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame

Kobe Bryant was a legendary basketball player who spent his entire 20-year career with the Los Angeles Lakers. He was a five-time NBA champion, a two-time Olympic gold medalist, and an 18-time NBA All-Star. He was known for his incredible work ethic, his competitive spirit, and his ability to score in a variety of ways. He was also a devoted husband and father, and his tragic death in a helicopter crash in 2020 shocked the world.

"Kobe was a beloved member of the Lakers family and the NBA community. He was an incredible player, a dedicated teammate, and a true champion. His legacy will live on through the generations of players and fans he inspired."

-- NBA Commissioner Adam Silver
